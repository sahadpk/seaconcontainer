Empty Inventory on a FIFO Basis

At Seacon Container Terminal, we implement FIFO (First In, First Out) inventory management for empty containers to ensure optimal efficiency and minimize handling delays. By following a FIFO approach, we prioritize the use of older containers, ensuring they are moved or dispatched first, which helps maintain smooth operations and prevents container congestion in our storage yards.

Our FIFO system improves container flow, minimizes idle times, and reduces the risk of damage from prolonged storage. This approach is ideal for clients looking for a reliable, systematic way to manage their container inventory, ensuring that containers are ready for use without unnecessary delays. Our team monitors inventory levels closely and adjusts storage arrangements as needed to maximize container usage and availability.
